Last Minute Mushroom

Description

This recipe for a buttery, delicious, mushroom side dish couldn't be any simpler.

It goes well with fish fillets, baked chicken, steak, and just about any other main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1½ cups fresh button mushrooms, sliced
  • ½ teaspoon seasoned salt

Steps

  1. In a skillet, over medium heat, melt the butter. Add mushrooms, and sprinkle with seasoned salt. Cook, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms soften and become darker in color, about 10 minutes.
